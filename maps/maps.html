SPLIT

On this map, the occupation of heights is of great importance. The two points are separated from each other by a center with high positions, which can be quickly climbed by ropes. Control over each point will help to keep the towers towering above them. Don't forget to look up.

BIND

Two dots. No foreign ministry. Where to go, to the right or to the left? Whatever you choose, in each direction there is a direct route to attack and a one-way teleport to get behind the backs of opponents.

HAVEN

The walls of the forgotten monastery are shaken by the noise of agents fighting for control of three points. There are more territories to capture, but it is much easier for defenders to aggressively push the enemy.

ASCENT

Two Ascent points are separated from each other by an open area for small positional skirmishes and shooting opponents. Each point can be reinforced with sealed doors; after activating them, you will need to either destroy them or find another way to the point. Not a step back!

ICEBOX

You have to fight on an abandoned secret Arctic base Kingdom. The points for placing the bomb are protected by snow and metal. Here you will need cunning and dexterity. Use the cables to go unnoticed or take the enemy by surprise.

BREEZE

Welcome to the tropical paradise. Admire ancient ruins, explore sea caves and bring friends with you. Here you will find an open area and shootouts at long distances, so there will definitely be no extra help. The main thing is to cover the flanks in time.

FRACTURE

A secret research center, split in two because of a failed experiment with a radiant. This unusual map opens up various opportunities for defenders: catch the attackers by surprise on their side or batten down the hatches to survive the assault. The choice is yours!
