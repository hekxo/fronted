Name: Phoenix
Role: Duelist
Biography: 
        Hailing from the U.K., Phoenix's star power shines through
        in his fighting style, igniting the
        battlefield with flash and flare.
        Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush
        into a fight on his own terms.
